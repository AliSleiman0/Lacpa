Notice No. 60 - Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorism Financing Procedures
In compliance with the requirements for combating money laundering and terrorism financing, as well as the applicable laws and regulations, and in response to the requirements of the Financial Action Task Force for the Middle East and North Africa (MENA FATF), the Lebanese Association of Certified Public Accountants reminds its members of the necessity to adhere to the provisions of these laws and regulations, particularly Law No. 2015/44 on Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorism Financing.
Ensure that your client is not listed on the local sanctions list (the national list issued by the Internal Security Forces and the UN sanctions lists issued by the Security Council).
Based on the above, please find below a table of electronic links that allow you to verify that none of your clients are listed on any of these lists.

UN Sanctions list 1267 at link: https://www.un.org/securitycouncil/en/sanctions/1267/aq_sanctions_list
UN Sanctions list 1988 at link: https://main.un.org/securitycouncil/en/sanctions/1988/materials
UN Sanctions list 1718 at link: https://main.un.org/securitycouncil/en/sanctions/1718/materials
National list at link: https://isf.gov.lb/ar/national-terrorism-financial-list
