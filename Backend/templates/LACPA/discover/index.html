| President Letter

Dear Members, Partners, and Visitors,
Welcome to the official website of the Lebanese Association of Certified Public Accountants (LACPA).

It is with great pride and a deep sense of responsibility that I address you as President of LACPA. Our Association stands at a pivotal moment in its history: one shaped by growing global challenges, technological transformation, and a renewed focus on integrity, transparency, and public trust.

LACPA’s mission has always been to uphold the highest standards in the accounting profession in Lebanon and to act as a key pillar in promoting financial stability, accountability, and sustainable economic development. Today, we remain committed to that mission, while embracing change and driving progress.

Our strategic vision centers on three pillars:

Professional Excellence: Enhancing the competencies of our members through high-quality education, continuous professional development, and alignment with international standards such as IFRS, ISAs, and ISQMs.

Public Interest and Ethics: Safeguarding the public interest through strict adherence to our Code of Ethics, promoting good governance, and reinforcing our profession’s role in protecting the credibility of financial information.

Collaboration and Innovation: Strengthening partnerships with national and international bodies, while embracing innovation and digital transformation to ensure our profession remains relevant and forward-looking.

Protection of Members' Interests: Advocating for members' rights, particularly in the absence of clear regulation to safeguard them against liabilities arising from negligence or gross negligence. We are working to enhance social security coverage, insurance protection, and retirement planning for our members. As part of these efforts, LACPA has taken the initiative to significantly increase financial contributions to the pension fund and improve retirement benefits. Furthermore, we have introduced a new law to establish the Professional Investigation Authority, which is empowered to grant permission to prosecute auditors in cases involving professional misconduct or negligence.

This website serves as a gateway to our services, updates, publications, and initiatives. Whether you are a member seeking resources, a student exploring the profession, or an institutional partner, we invite you to engage with us, participate in our activities, and contribute to the development of the accountancy profession in Lebanon. Thank you for your trust and continued support.

Warm regards,
Elie Abboud
President
